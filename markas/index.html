How does it work?

Here are the simple steps we’ll ask you to take if you decide to help us in the fight against food waste.
During the meal
Scan the QR code you’ll find on the table tents at the center of the table.
The first time you log in, you’ll be asked to create your personal profile.
Tell us how it went!
Answer a very short feedback survey about any leftovers on your tray.
It’s so quick you’ll want to do it every day! 😉
Collect tickets
Each feedback you provide gives you the chance to win a tiny treat! To learn more, check the official rules at this link.
A WebApp just for you
You can access the webapp anytime to track your canteen's progress and see how we're improving together!
