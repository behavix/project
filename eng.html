Our approach

Behavix is an innovative startup created to put
an end to waste in organized catering.
How much food is wasted in canteens?


Did you know that in Europe alone, every day we leave enough food on our plates to feed 2,000,000 people?
Behavix’s mission, as a Benefit Company, is to fight this waste!

Why start from plate leftovers?


That’s where waste takes shape!
Most food is lost on plates: uneaten food that could feed those in need.

We tackle food waste at its root!


Understanding the reasons behind leftovers is key to taking action, reducing waste throughout the entire supply chain.

Smarter collective catering!

  1. Real-time monitoring of waste, across all locations distributed in the territory.
  2. Cause analysis: user engagement and data correlation from multiple sources reveal what really generates waste.
  3. Clear and immediate data, easy to interpret and ready to be turned into concrete actions.
  4. Machine learning algorithms to guide the planning of targeted prevention measures.
