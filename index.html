Our aim: zero leftovers on canteen trays!

At Behavix we work to optimize the quantity of food prepared in canteens and to eliminate leftovers from the trays at the end of the meal!

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Thanks to our algorithm we assist users over time and create a digital twin of their appetite level. We use data for accurate forecasts to serve users according to their needs.
This way leftovers will be gone for good!

We assist users and catering companies through the whole process:
PRE-SERVICE

While reserving their meals users are guided towards the choice of balanced portions

MEAL SERVICE

When at the canteen, users still get to specify some preferences and are served accordingly

FEEDBACK

Once the meal is over, users are asked to provide a feedback about quantities and reasons for leftovers

Technology & User engagement
to determine the DDP (Data-Driven Portion)

Data-Driven Portion is the core of Behavix algorithm. Based on the quantities of foodwaste detected by the system, the algorithm is able to use data to tailor food portions for each user and every course at the canteen. 
Each of the tailor-made portions takes the name of Data-Driven Portion.
